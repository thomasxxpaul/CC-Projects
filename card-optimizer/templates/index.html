Chase Trifecta Tip
Since you have both CSP + CFU, you can transfer Freedom Unlimited points into your Sapphire Preferred account and redeem them at 2¢ per point via travel partners. That turns CFU's 1.5x flat rate into an effective 3% back on everything — better than most dedicated cashback cards.
🏠
Bilt Rent Tip
Always pay rent with Bilt. It's the only card that earns points on rent with zero transaction fee. At 1x points worth ~1.7¢ each, paying $2,000/month in rent = ~$408/year in travel value for free. No minimum transaction requirement.

🔔 How to never miss a transfer bonus
📧
Frequent Miler newsletter — the best free email alert for transfer bonuses. They email within hours of a new bonus launching. Sign up at frequentmiler.com
📱
Bilt app push notifications — enable notifications in the Bilt app to get alerted on Rent Day bonus events and transfer promotions directly from Bilt.
✉️
Chase promotional emails — log in to chase.com → Profile → Communication preferences → opt in to "Rewards offers & promotions" to get UR transfer bonus emails.
🛍️
Rakuten bonus events — Rakuten runs seasonal "Big Fat Check" events where you earn 10x+ points at restaurants or stores. Free to join at rakuten.com — they email enrolled members when events start.
✈️
Airline & hotel apps — United, Hyatt, and Marriott sometimes run their own "transfer to us and get 30% more" promos. Enable push notifications in each loyalty app to catch these — they often last only 1–2 weeks.
